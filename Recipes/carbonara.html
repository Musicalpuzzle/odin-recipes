Pasta carbonara

Description

Classic recipe for creamy Italian spaghetti carbonara with bacon, eggs and Parmesan cheese

For 4 persons

  • 400 gr spaghetti
  • 200 gr smoked bacon strips or cubes
  • Fresh parsley
  • 100 gr Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 3 eggs
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

  1. Fry the bacon in a skillet until they are nice and crisp.
  2. In the meantime, bring a large pan of water with a pinch of salt to a boil and add the spaghetti. Cook the spaghetti for around 10 mins until al dente. (or check the packaging for cooking times)
  3. Add the pasta directly from the pan into the skillet and mix with the bacon. (Save some of the water in the pan) Turn off the gas.
  4. Add the parmesan cheese to the skillet and add a little water from the spaghetti you cooked earlier, to make sure the cheese melts.
  5. Whisk the eggs and add them whilst stirring to the skillet, keep stirring until it becomes a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve with a little cheese and parsley
  7. Enjoy!
